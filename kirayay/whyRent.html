Why Rent?

  • Clothing, Electronics and Furniture industries together are resonsible for more than 30% of pollution resonsible for climate change.
  • Consumers today buy more items and keep them forlesser time than they used to a couple of decades ago.
  • The best we can do to escape this Buy. Use. Throw away. cycle is to limit our consumption and extract more from stuff we already have.
